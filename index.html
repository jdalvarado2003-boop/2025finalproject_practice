Who Are the X-Men?

The X-Men are a legendary team of mutants dedicated to protecting a world that often fears and misunderstands them. Formed by Professor Charles Xavier, the group stands for justice, unity, and hope for both humans and mutants.

Famous Members

  • Professor X – Founder and telepathic leader
  • Wolverine – Regeneration and adamantium claws
  • Storm – Weather-controlling powerhouse
  • Cyclops – Optic blast strategist
  • Jean Grey – Omega-level telepath and Phoenix host
